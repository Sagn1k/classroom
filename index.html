Who Are We?

We are a group of people who have been working with friends and family, to support intellectual development of children from underprivileged strata of the society.

While we have been dedicatedly working with children offline since 2019, due to the devastating pandemic, we have decided to move our operations online and increase our reach. In the past, we have supported children with healthcare and education, and we intend to expand the same such that every child can attain minimum educational qualification without any struggle

What Do We Do?

We want to create a channel for kind-hearted people to reach and help children in need. On this platform, we have enabled two options: Lend A Hand, and Get Support. Those who are in urgent need of funds can fill the form under Get Support segment. Our team will run a background check, verify the details and connect you to the concerned donor.

Similarly, those who want to donate can click on Lend A Hand, and donate through our fundraiser in collaboration with the Milaap Foundation.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between the donor and the recipient.
